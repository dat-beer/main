ölat 5.5%

Hybrid theory in practice, ölat is a rice and oat lager originally crafted by Kristofer and Dat with a 150-liter brew batch in Lomma, between December 2024 and March 2025. Now brewed regularly at Kävlinge Brewery with great help from Per, ölat's unique blend showcases a crisp, refreshing taste. The percentage of oat in the recipe is projected to increase by one percent per year, enhancing its smooth character over time.
