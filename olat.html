ölat 5.5%

is a hybrid theory in practice. The idea of introducing oat into rice beer was suggested by Kristofer to improve the mouthfeel and color of the beer while maintaining the gluten-free attribute. Dat thought that it is fair to include oat as an counterpart to rice from Vietnam and decide to use 5% oat in the grain bills to commemorate his fifth year in Sweden. With a great selection of New Zealand hops proposed by Kristofer, ölat tastes like a summer breeze. ölat is now regularly brewed at Kävlinge Brewery with great help from Per. The label is designed by Trang Hà. For the future of ölat, the percentage of oat is projected to increase by one percent every year.

Ingredients: water, rice, oat, yeast, hops.
Contributors: Kristofer Eklundh, Per Daneskog, Trang Ha Truong Nguyen, Dat Nguyen Trong.
