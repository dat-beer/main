dat 5.5%

A diasporic manifesto, dat is a recipe-fluid rice beer crafted in a 5-liter batch in Lund in April 2024, followed by 60-liter batches in Brösarp between June and November 2024, a 10-liter batch in Den Haag in December 2024, and now in 150-liter batches at Kävlinge Brewery. Celebrated at the closing of Cow Girl Gallery, the opening of Black Archive Sweden, Truedafest Festival 2024, and pop-ups at Dåndimpen, 234+ Studio, Aile House, and Haanddat, dat embodies a vibrant, evolving spirit.
